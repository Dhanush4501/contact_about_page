    GeSeven Medica, a leading pharmaceutical company located in Petaling Jaya, Selangor, was established in November 2007. The company's primary business is trading in medicines, medical devices, and medical equipment for clinics and hospitals. GeSeven Medica has been a well-known enterprise since its inception and has been serving private clinics and hospitals in the Klang Valley. It was founded by Mr. Nadarajan Govindasamy, the sole proprietor in Damansara Utama, Petaling Jaya, who successfully developed the business to serve approximately 500 customers as of 2013. He continues to apply his 20 years of experience in the medical field to further expand his business.
